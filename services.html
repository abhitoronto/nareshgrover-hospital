What We Offer

Our Clinics & Services

Comprehensive pediatric care under one roof — from well-baby checks to intensive neonatal and surgical care.

Well Baby Vaccination Clinic

Our vaccination clinic follows the national immunization schedule to protect every child against preventable diseases. We provide a safe, child-friendly environment for immunizations, with experienced staff who know how to put children at ease.

  • Complete national immunization schedule
  • Travel vaccines and catch-up programs
  • Vaccination records and reminders
  • Counseling for parents on vaccine safety

Growth Monitoring Clinic

Systematic, ongoing monitoring of every child’s physical and developmental milestones. Early detection of growth concerns allows for timely intervention, ensuring every child achieves their full potential.

  • Height, weight, and head circumference tracking
  • WHO growth chart assessments
  • Developmental milestone evaluation
  • Nutritional guidance and referrals

Nutrition & Wellness Clinic

Led by Tanya Grover, a Certified Lactation Educator, this clinic offers personalized nutritional counseling for children of all ages and expert lactation support for new mothers. Good nutrition is the foundation of a healthy childhood.

  • Breastfeeding and lactation support
  • Infant and toddler feeding guidance
  • Diet plans for underweight or overweight children
  • Management of nutritional deficiencies

Neuro Development Clinic

Dedicated assessment and long-term management of children with neurological and developmental concerns. Our team works closely with families to create individualized care plans and coordinate with therapists and educators.

  • Epilepsy diagnosis and management
  • Autism spectrum disorder evaluation
  • ADHD assessment and management
  • Developmental delay screening
  • Cerebral palsy and movement disorders

Allergy & Asthma Clinic

Dr. Naresh Grover is Punjab’s first pediatrician to complete a fellowship in allergy & immunology (2018) and pioneered Skin Prick Testing in the region. We offer precise diagnosis and evidence-based management of allergic and respiratory conditions.

  • Skin Prick Testing for allergen identification
  • Asthma diagnosis and management plans
  • Food allergy evaluation
  • Eczema and urticaria management
  • Allergic rhinitis treatment

Adolescent Clinic (Ages 10–19)

Teenagers have unique health needs that differ from both children and adults. Our adolescent clinic provides a confidential, supportive environment for young people to discuss their health concerns freely, with guidance tailored to this critical stage of development.

  • Puberty-related concerns and guidance
  • Nutritional needs during growth spurts
  • Mental health and behavioural concerns
  • Acne, skin, and hormonal issues
  • Sports medicine and musculoskeletal care
