About Us

Compassionate Care
for Every Child

“Be a child to treat a child — helping every child feel safe and sound.”

Dr. Naresh Grover Hospital was established in 1999 with just 12 beds and a single mission: delivering quality pediatric care to children of all sections of society at reasonable cost. Over the past 25 years, we have grown into a 40-bed advanced neonatal and pediatric center, continually upgrading our facilities and equipment.

We introduced Amritsar’s first Hypothermia Cradle for severe birth asphyxia, and were first in Punjab to offer fellowship-level allergy & immunology expertise with Skin Prick Testing. Our team combines deep experience with genuine compassion for every patient.

What We Offer

Our Clinics & Services

Comprehensive pediatric and neonatal care under one roof, staffed by specialists dedicated to your child’s health.

Vaccination Clinic

Complete well-baby vaccination programs following the national immunization schedule, ensuring every child is protected from preventable diseases.

Growth Monitoring Clinic

Systematic tracking of physical and developmental milestones to detect and address growth concerns early.

Nutrition & Wellness Clinic

Led by Tanya Grover (Certified Lactation Educator), providing personalized nutrition counseling and wellness guidance for children and mothers.

Neuro Development Clinic

Specialized assessment and management of epilepsy, autism spectrum disorder, ADHD, and developmental delays.

Allergy & Asthma Clinic

Pioneering Skin Prick Testing for precise pediatric allergy diagnosis, backed by Punjab’s first fellowship-trained allergy specialist.

NICU 10 Beds

State-of-the-art Neonatal Intensive Care Unit with hypothermia cradles, CPAP, ventilators, surfactant therapy, and round-the-clock neonatal specialists.

PICU 6 Beds

Pediatric Intensive Care Unit equipped with advanced monitoring, infusion pumps, and a dedicated team for critically ill children.

Pediatric Surgery

Specialized surgical care for children led by Dr. Sumit Aggarwal (MS, MCh from PGI), covering a wide range of pediatric surgical conditions.

Adolescent Clinic

Dedicated care for patients aged 10–19, addressing the unique physical, emotional, and developmental needs of adolescents.
