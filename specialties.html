Newborn Intensive Care

Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)

10-Bed Unit · 24/7 Specialist Cover

Our 10-bed NICU is the most advanced neonatal care facility in the region. Established progressively since 1999, it was the first in Amritsar to introduce Hypothermia Cradle therapy for severe birth asphyxia — a treatment that dramatically improves outcomes for affected newborns.

We accept referrals from across the region for the full spectrum of neonatal emergencies, with modern monitoring equipment and a dedicated team of neonatologists available at all times.

Conditions We Treat

Premature / Low Birth Weight Birth Asphyxia Respiratory Distress Syndrome Neonatal Jaundice Neonatal Seizures Sepsis & Infections Bleeding Disorders Poor Feeding Bilious Vomiting Congenital Anomalies

Equipment & Capabilities

  • Hypothermia Cradles (first in Amritsar)
  • CPAP & Mechanical Ventilators
  • Surfactant Therapy
  • Pulse Oximeters & Continuous Monitors
  • Infusion Pumps & NIBP Monitors
  • Phototherapy Units

Critical Pediatric Care

Paediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU)

6-Bed Unit · Latest Equipment

Our 6-bed PICU is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by experienced pediatric intensivists. We manage the most critically ill children with precision, compassion, and urgency — giving every child the best possible chance of recovery.

Conditions We Manage

Respiratory Distress Meningitis & Encephalitis Bronchopneumonia Septic Shock Status Epilepticus Diabetic Ketoacidosis Severe Dehydration Post-surgical Monitoring

Equipment & Capabilities

  • Mechanical Ventilators
  • Continuous Multi-parameter Monitoring
  • Infusion Pumps & Syringe Drivers
  • NIBP & Pulse Oximetry
  • Central Line & Arterial Line Capability

Surgical Expertise

Paediatric Surgery Department

Led by Dr. Sumit Aggarwal · MS, MCh (PGI)

Our Pediatric Surgery Department is led by Dr. Sumit Aggarwal, trained at the prestigious Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education & Research (PGI). We offer the full spectrum of pediatric surgical care, from routine procedures to highly complex neonatal surgery.

Every child receives a customized surgical plan with the goal of minimizing risk, reducing recovery time, and ensuring the best long-term outcome.

Routine Procedures

  • Tongue Tie (Ankyloglossia)
  • Phimosis Correction
  • Inguinal & Umbilical Hernias
  • Appendicitis
  • Circumcision
  • Minor wound repairs

Complex Surgeries

  • Tracheoesophageal Fistula
  • Imperforate Anus
  • Diaphragmatic Hernia
  • Intestinal Atresia
  • Pyloric Stenosis
  • Hirschsprung’s Disease
